Amazing Science Facts

Fascinating facts from your curriculum topics to make you go "Wow!"

🌿

Living Things

There are more than 8.7 million different species of living things on Earth — and scientists are still discovering new ones every year!

🌻

Plants

A single large tree can absorb up to 22 kilograms of carbon dioxide per year and produce enough oxygen for two people to breathe!

🐙

Animals

An elephant's trunk has over 40,000 individual muscles — more than an entire human body — making it incredibly strong and precise.

❤

Human Body

Your heart beats about 100,000 times every single day. Over a lifetime, that is more than 2.5 billion heartbeats!

🌍

Environment

The ocean covers more than 70% of Earth's surface and contains about 97% of all the water on our planet.

☀

Solar System

The Sun is so enormous that approximately one million Earths could fit inside it. Yet it is considered an average-sized star!

Brain Teasers

Science Riddles

I am green, I use sunlight, and I make my own food. What am I?

A plant! Plants are the only living things that can make their own food through photosynthesis.

I pump 5 litres of blood around your body every minute. What am I?

The heart! This powerful muscle keeps you alive by circulating blood non-stop, day and night.

I have no fixed shape and I fill any container completely. What state of matter am I?

A gas! Gas particles spread out to fill whatever container they are in. Air in a balloon is a great example.

I fall from clouds as rain, flow to rivers, evaporate, and start the cycle over again. What am I?

The water cycle! Water constantly moves between clouds, rain, rivers, and evaporation in a never-ending cycle.
